Learn

There is no such thing as "digital arrest"

No real agency — police, CBI, ED, Customs — ever arrests you over a video call or asks you to stay online.

They create panic, then isolate you

Scammers accuse you of a crime, threaten arrest, and tell you to tell no one. That isolation is the trap.

No agency asks for money to "verify"

Never transfer funds to a "safe" or "RBI" account, and never share an OTP — no matter who they claim to be.
